Office Tracker Contact Manager

Free 30-Day Trial

Office Tracker's easy to use integrated contact management system lets you share and schedule your customers and other contacts - company wide - in a great address book format.

Never hurt for crucial business information again. With Office Tracker Contact Manager you can locate any customer and every appointment or meeting you've had with that customer, in one click. If a customer calls to check an appointment time or wants to set up another meeting, you can quickly give them an answer without having to seach or run a report. Everything you need is right at your fingertips.

The Office Tracker Contact manager makes it easier than ever to stay on top of your customers, patients, vendors, employees and others. You can easily schedule any contact into the Office Tracker calendar by dragging and dropping the contact icon or by adding it via the Contact button in the New Event window.

  • Share Contacts with everyone in your office
  • Schedule contacts with one click - the name and phone number are automatically entered in your event description
  • Eliminate double-bookings
  • Maintain a detailed history of every appointment, meeting or event scheduled with contacts
  • Import contacts from Outlook and other applications
  • Print envelopes, mailing labels and detailed reports
  • Find contact information instanly
  • Track customized information about each contact based on your specific needs
