Frequently Asked Questions & Answers

Q. Can Office Tracker be used to schedule meetings, facilities and rooms?

A. Yes. Office Tracker is designed to schedule meetings, rooms, facilities and resources as well as people, appointments and events. You can view side-by-side any number of these names and see complete schedule information with full text(not just color blocks). It's the most flexible office scheduling software available.

Q. Can we all share schedule information in Office Tracker?

A. Yes. Office Tracker lets you set 'sharing privileges" so users can schedule for one another, see schedules of rooms, resources and other co-workers and notify users of upcoming meetings and events. You can even set reminders for other users, provided you've set up privileges at the Office Tracker Server to do so.

Q. Can I create report in Office Tracker?

A. Yes. Office Tracker allows you to create many printed and on screen time used reports. You can print daily, weekly, monthly, detailed list wiich is a columnar report format you can customize with particular information. You can print in combinations with any number of names included. You can print group schedules with names side-by-side in Daily format as well.

In addition, you can do a "Find" and export the "found" events (all events within the month of June with a particular label for instance). The exported event information can be easily opened in a word processor, spreadsheet or databas application for further customizaion.

Q. Can I send e-mail reminders to customers and others?

A. Yes, Office Tracker lets you send automated reminders and notifications to customers and others whether they are part of the Office Tracker users in your office or not.

Can I integrate Office Tracker with our Outlook or other e-mail system?

You can choose to receive event notifications like meeting requests and reminders through your Outlook e-mail or other e-mail programs.

Q. Can I create repeating (recurring meetins and other events?

A. Yes, you can set any meeting, appointment or other event to recur on a Daily, Weekly, Monthly or Yearly basis.

Q. How do I add rooms to the Office Tracker free trial?

A. You add names for rooms, people and other resources in the Office Tracker Server Admin uder the "Names List" option as documented in Quick Start that was e-mailed to you.

Q. What is the Office Tracker database engine written in?

A. The Office Tracker multi-user server ships pre-configured to use the database Hypersonic SQL. If you want to use a JDBC data source for the Office Tracker server database such as Oracle, MSSQL or MySQL contact Office Tracker for more information: 1-512-469-2967

Q. Is Office Tracker Web-based?

A. Offie Tracker offers you the choices of a web-based or client/server based solution. Office Tracker can be configured to allow you to connect from remote locations and stay up to date with schedule changes if you are on te road, in another office, or even in another country.
